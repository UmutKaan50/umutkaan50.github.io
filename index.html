About Me

Umut Kaan Özdemir, a committed Computer Scientist and Game Developer from Türkiye, began his passion for gaming as a child, leading him to a computer science degree and a career in game development. Embarking on his programming journey in 2019, he faced challenges but persevered to master his skills. In 2021, he transitioned into game development with Unity, marking a pivotal turning point in his career. Currently, he is expanding his knowledge into 3D modeling, animation, and computer graphics to create even more compelling gaming experiences.

Umut Kaan is not only interested in crafting games for entertainment but is also deeply driven by a desire to provide thought-provoking, enriching experiences that promote personal growth among players. His games aim to impart wisdom and highlight truths, proving that gaming is more than just amusement; it's an interactive medium for meaningful growth.
